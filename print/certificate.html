The Marriage Procession

Rajasthani Miniature · Natural Mineral Pigments · Silk

Verified Original  ·  Pacific Art Catalogue PAC — 2026 — 003

What you are looking at took 28 days to make and four generations to become possible. It was painted in natural mineral pigments — lapis from Afghanistan, malachite from Central Asia, ochre from nearby earth — ground by hand in stone mortars, on silk, using brushes made from a single hair of Siberian red squirrel fur. There is no other one like it. There never will be.
